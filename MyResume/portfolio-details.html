    About the Capstone Project

    In this project, we will be making a prototype of a smart eyeglass that can help a visually challenged person to recognize the person in front of him, learn about obstacles ahead, take voice commands to get navigation from Google maps and has the ability to read a book.

    About the EPICS Project

    Human activity recognition (HAR) is an exceptionally powerful and testing research theme. It targets deciding the exercises of an individual or a gathering of people dependent on sensor or potentially video perception information, just as on information about the setting inside which the noticed exercises happen. In the ideal case, a movement is perceived paying little heed to the climate it is acted in or the performing individual.
